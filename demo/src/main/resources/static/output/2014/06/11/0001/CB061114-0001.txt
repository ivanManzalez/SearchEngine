<HEADLINE>
<P>
Quebec's Nova Bus gets $32.6M order from TTC; 
</P>
<P>
Company to supply Toronto Transit Commission with 55 diesel buses
</P>
</HEADLINE>
<TEXT>
<P>
Quebec-based Nova Bus says it has received a $32.6-million order for diesel buses from the Toronto Transit Commission.
</P>
<P>
The transit bus manufacturer says it will supply the Toronto transit authority with 55 buses with delivery set to begin in 2015.
</P>
<P>
Nova Bus says the contract strengthens its presence in Ontario.
</P>
<P>
The buses will be entirely assembled at the company's main plant in St-Eustache, near Montreal, and will include a specially-designed barrier to help protect drivers made from steel and tempered glass.
</P>
<P>
Nova Bus is a leading manufacturer of transit vehicles, including electric-hybrid, natural gas and bus rapid transit vehicles.
</P>
<P>
It's part of the Volvo Group.
</P>
