<HEADLINE>
<P>
2nd-degree murder charge laid in Hammonds Plains homicide; 
</P>
<P>
Nova Scotia RCMP have charged Stephen Beckett, 45, in relation to the death of Tracey MacKenzie
</P>
</HEADLINE>
<TEXT>
<P>
Nova Scotia RCMP have charged Stephen Beckett, 45, of Hammonds Plains, N.S., with second-degree murder in the death of Tracey MacKenzie.
</P>
<P>
Officers were called to a home on Glen Arbour Way around 2:40 p.m. Thursday where they found MacKenzie's body.
</P>
<P>
They arrested Beckett at the scene. He was taken into custody without incident, according to a press release.
</P>
<P>
Chris Hansen, spokesperson for the Public Prosecution Service, called the case a domestic homicide.
</P>
<P>
The RCMP investigation into the incident is continuing.
</P>
<P>
Police are asking for the public's help to get more information on the case. Halifax District RCMP can be contacted directly at 902-490-5020. Anonymous tips can be shared with Nova Scotia Crime Stoppers at 1-800-222-TIPS (8477).
</P>
