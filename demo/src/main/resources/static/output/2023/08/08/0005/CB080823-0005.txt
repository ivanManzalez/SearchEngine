<HEADLINE>
<P>
Man, 26, dead following aggravated assault in downtown Halifax; 
</P>
<P>
The man was found unresponsive in the waterfront area off Lower Water Street
</P>
</HEADLINE>
<TEXT>
<P>
A 26-year-old man has died following an aggravated assault that happened Sunday night in downtown Halifax.
</P>
<P>
A news release said Halifax Regional Police received reports of a disturbance in the waterfront area of the 1500 block of Lower Water Street around 9:40 p.m.
</P>
<P>
Officers found an unresponsive man on the boardwalk.
</P>
<P>
He was taken to hospital with life-threatening injuries, but later died.
</P>
<P>
Investigators have not released the name of the victim.
</P>
<P>
"A further update on the investigation will be provided following the completion of work being conducted by the Medical Examiner Service," a news release said.
</P>
<P>
Police are asking anyone with information about the incident or video from the area at the time to come forward.
</P>
