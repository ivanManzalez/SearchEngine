<HEADLINE>
<P>
Missing Pictou County man found safe; 
</P>
<P>
77-year-old man was reported missing Tuesday
</P>
</HEADLINE>
<TEXT>
<P>
A 77-year-old man who was reported missing in Kings Head, N.S., on Tuesday, has been found safe.
</P>
<P>
The man was reported missing after he told his family he was going for a walk but didn't return.
</P>
<P>
Pictou County RCMP said local ground search and rescue teams, a RCMP police dog team and RCMP members searched for the man throughout the night.
</P>
<P>
Police reported that the man was found safe early Wednesday.
</P>
